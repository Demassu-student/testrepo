What is User Story?
A concise statement about a feature made from the perspective of the user, a well-defined user story describes the user's goals rather than the specific functionality. User stories are an important aspect of the design process because they humanize the design. They're told from the user's perspective, and they're used to inspire and guide design decisions. Source: "What are User Stories" by Interaction Design Foundation

User Story
As a business owner who has little time in planning and searches up to add a new destination for my where-to-destination list, I want to get suggestions of a set of business vacation trip (Workation) destinations coming up in 2022, so that I can focus to arrange my time to get there while still can managing my business during my business trip.
Reason
Seorang yang suka traveling diasumsikan sudah mempunyai daftar tujuan destinasinya sendiri yang dalam konteks ini ingin menambahkan daftar destinasi tujuannya namun spesifik pada liburan sambil bekerja atau bekerja dengan liburan. Dengan menggunakan konsep digital nomad, planning tempat strategis yang dapat mencakup mapping place(distance) dari tempat tempat penginapan, cafe, rental, objek wisata, dll beserta fitur-fitur, fasilitas maupun persyaratannya dapat menguras banyak waktu.
Microcopy
Microcopy(EN):
  • In-app Banner: Let's do a vacation while remote-work in 2022 (45)
  • Button: Get overview places (19)
Microcopy(ID):
  • In-app Banner: Mudah rencanakan liburan sambil kerja di 2022 (45)
  • Button: Cek tempat favoritmu(20)
